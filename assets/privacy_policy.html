Information We Do NOT Collect

LineGlide is fully offline. The following categories of data are never collected, stored on our servers, or transmitted in any form:

Personal identity Location / GPS Device identifiers Analytics / telemetry Crash reports Financial information Camera data Microphone data Contacts Browsing history Email address IP address

We have no back-end server infrastructure connected to the game. There is no account system, no login, and no cloud sync.

4

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide a proper game experience (saving progress, remembering your best score), LineGlide stores a small amount of data locally using Android/iOS SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

Data Item Purpose Storage Shared?
Current level number Resume progress between sessions On-device only Never
High score (metres) Display personal best On-device only Never
Game-in-progress flag Show "Continue" option on menu On-device only Never
Total distance travelled Cumulative progress tracking On-device only Never

We, Hamad Gaming Studio, have zero access to this data. It is managed entirely by your device's operating system.

Data Security

Since all data resides exclusively on your device, the security of that data is governed by your device's operating system, storage encryption, and screen-lock settings.

We strongly recommend keeping your device software up to date and using a screen lock to protect all data stored on your device, including LineGlide save data.

9

Deleting Your Data

You are in complete control of all locally stored game data. You can delete it at any time using any of the methods below:

In-app reset: Tap "New Game" on the main menu to wipe all level progress and start fresh.
App settings: Go to your device Settings → Apps → LineGlide → Storage → Clear Data.
Uninstall: Removing LineGlide from your device permanently deletes all stored data.
